# E-Commerce-Data-Visualization-in-Power-BI-
Power BI Ecommerce Dashboard analyzes sales, profit, quantity, and shipping costs across countries, states, categories, and markets. Interactive visuals reveal top products and regions, shipping efficiency, and market trends—empowering business decisions with actionable insights.

**E-Commerce Data Visualization in Power BI **
It showcases key Insights :
 1. Office Supplies lead in sales, but Technology and Furniture follow closely.
 2. USCA market and England are top revenue drivers.
 3. Standard Class shipping dominates, indicating customer preference for cost-effective delivery.
 4. Despite high sales, profit margins remain relatively modest—highlighting room for optimization.
